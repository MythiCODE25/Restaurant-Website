ABOUT

"About Byte & Bite Where Tech Meets Taste. At Byte & Bite, we bring together two passions — great food and modern innovation. Designed for food lovers, creators, and tech enthusiasts alike, our café blends vibrant flavors with a cool digital vibe. From gourmet burgers and crispy fries to handcrafted coffees and desserts, every bite is made to power your day. Whether you’re here to chill, work, or connect, Byte & Bite is your space to refuel, recharge, and relax. Welcome to the future of flavor — one byte at a time."
